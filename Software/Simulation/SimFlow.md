# SimFlow

## Overview

SimFlow is a user-focused CFD application with practical pump capabilities including centrifugal and axial pump cases. It is commonly chosen for teams wanting an accessible interface rather than a fully scripted stack.

---

## Core concepts

- GUI-first setup for fluid cases.
- Supports dedicated pump and turbomachinery case workflows.
- Typically used for early-stage screening, reporting-ready outputs, and medium-scale studies.

---

## Pros

- Lower barrier to entry than code-first CFD engines.
- Fast campaign launch for repeatable pump studies.
- Useful for teams with mixed analysis maturity.

## Cons

- Less transparent internals than open frameworks.
- Limited flexibility when deeply custom automation is required.
- May require additional tools for enterprise-wide lifecycle governance.

---

## Use cases

- Candidate geometry screening before high-fidelity handoff.
- Internal pump performance reviews in small to medium programs.
- Supporting engineering teams that prefer guided workflows.
