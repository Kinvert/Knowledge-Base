# Simcenter STAR-CCM+

## Overview

Simcenter STAR-CCM+ is a Siemens industrial CFD platform with broad rotating-machinery capabilities. It is frequently used for turbine, compressor, and pump studies where industrial process integration and traceability matter.

---

## Core concepts

- Enterprise multiphysics environment with motion and rotating-body support.
- Large-project governance features for regulated environments.
- Often paired with Siemens training and platform-specific rollout patterns.

---

## Pros

- Strong motion treatment and robust industrial solver coverage.
- Good fit for long-running, auditable design programs.
- Broad post-processing and coupling behavior compared with smaller apps.

## Cons

- Expensive compared with lighter alternatives.
- Requires trained teams to avoid steep onboarding cost.
- Complex setup can slow very early exploratory work.

---

## Use cases

- Industrial rotating-machinery programs with governance and traceability requirements.
- Rotordynamic and high-speed multiphysics studies needing stable enterprise workflows.
- Teams standardized on Siemens process integration and training.
