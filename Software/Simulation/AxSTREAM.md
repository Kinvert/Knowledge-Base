# AxSTREAM

## Overview

AxSTREAM is a multi-module SoftInWay platform for turbomachinery design, analysis, and optimization, including pump applications alongside compressors and turbines.

---

## Core concepts

- Integrates geometry, performance prediction, and system-level modules.
- Supports workflows spanning concept, design, mapping, and optimization.
- Useful where teams want fewer point tools and more unified process continuity.

---

## Pros

- Strong end-to-end coverage for turbomachinery programs.
- Better cross-module continuity than tightly siloed tools.
- Good option when pump work is part of a larger machine product line.

## Cons

- Broad capability comes with setup overhead.
- Smaller teams may find deployment weight high.
- Requires structured governance to avoid module sprawl.

---

## Use cases

- Programs requiring one environment from meanline to system coupling.
- Enterprises coordinating multiple pump families and related turbomachinery families.
- Teams needing long-horizon design reproducibility and internal consistency.
