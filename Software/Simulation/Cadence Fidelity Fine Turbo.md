# Cadence Fidelity Fine Turbo

## Overview

Fidelity Fine Turbo is Cadence’s rotor and turbomachinery analysis suite focused on performance, efficiency, and optimization in rotating machinery.

---

## Core concepts

- Purpose-built for rotating-fluid performance prediction.
- Emphasizes cycle-efficient studies and design optimization.
- Positioned as a specialized alternative to general-purpose industrial CFD for turbomachinery.

---

## Pros

- Deep domain focus on turbine/compressor/rotor behavior.
- Stronger performance-driven workflows than broad multiphysics suites in many pump contexts.
- Useful for teams already aligned with Cadence workflows.

## Cons

- Narrower domain breadth outside rotating machinery.
- Not always best for broader thermal-coupled multi-physics projects.
- Vendor lock-in and licensing overhead can be significant.

---

## Use cases

- Turbomachinery performance optimization where rotational effects dominate.
- Design-space refinement for efficiency gains across operating points.
- Teams using Cadence-driven performance and geometry loops.
