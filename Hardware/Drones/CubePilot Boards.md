# CubePilot Boards

`CubePilot` products sit between strict Pixhawk-standard boards and fully custom companion stacks, with `Cube Orange+` as the common flagship in this vault.

---

## CubePilot controller shortlist

| Board | Main processor | Why teams pick it | Common caveat |
|---|---|---|---|
| [[Cube Orange+]] | `STM32H757` (dual-core M7+M4) | Strong mission uptime behavior and ecosystem tooling depth | Connector/stack compatibility differs from generic Pixhawk standards |

---

## Stack-level direction

- Use `[[ArduPilot Boards]]` and `[[PX4 Boards]]` for stack-first comparisons and cross-stack compatibility trade-offs.
- `Cube Orange+` is frequently treated as a field-robust modular option more than a strict price-only benchmark.
