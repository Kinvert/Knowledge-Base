# Holybro Pixhawk 6C Mini

`Holybro Pixhawk 6C Mini` is the compact mini packaging version of Holybro’s FMUv6C family, still centered on `STM32H743`.

---

## Processor and board position

- FMU processor: `STM32H743`
- Same FMUv6C-family class as larger 6C variants, with tighter mechanical footprint
- Typical use: compact multirotor, fixed-wing or rover boards where volume is a hard constraint

---

## Practical strengths

- Smaller frame/fusion footprint than full 6C
- Lower power path complexity in compact builds
- Useful bridge between hobby-scale and compact production-style builds

---

## Practical caveats

- Smaller mechanical margin means cable routing and thermal layout deserve stronger review
- Companion and payload integration still follows standard Pixhawk-family best practices (serial mapping, bootloader target matching)
